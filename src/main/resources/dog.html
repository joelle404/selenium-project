About Dogs

Dogs are loyal and social animals, often considered "man's best friend." They have been domesticated for thousands of years and are known for their companionship and protective instincts.

Dogs communicate using a variety of sounds, including barks, growls, and whines. They are highly trainable and are used in various roles, such as hunting, herding, and providing emotional support.

As social animals, dogs thrive in environments where they receive plenty of attention and exercise.
