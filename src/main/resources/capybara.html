About Capybaras

Capybaras are large, social rodents native to South America. Known for their friendly nature, they are often found in groups and enjoy being in water.

Capybaras communicate through a series of clicks, whistles, and purrs. They are herbivores, feeding primarily on grasses and aquatic plants.

They have webbed feet, making them excellent swimmers. Capybaras are a favorite among animal enthusiasts due to their gentle and social behavior.
